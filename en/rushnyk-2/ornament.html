Grains above the tree crown

These are dreams and aspirations. They are also the seeds of a better future, a symbol of healing and new life that begins on the foundations of the past. They are sown to sprout soon.

Two doves

Symbol of a pair who seek to be reunited. The man and woman are symbolically placed on different branches, they look at each other, but there is distance and obstacles between them.

Tree of life

It symbolizes the life of a person and the life on their land, genetic memory, strong deep roots, and family. The tree trunk is twisted and chopped, telling the story of the search and the difficult tangled path that the heroes had to overcome.

Tree vase

It is an ancient symbol of ancestry and family. The ripples on the vase symbolize life. It's also just the sea, as the story unfolds in a city by the sea.
